##### Eulers Identity:

$$
e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0
$$

$$
e^{\frac{i\pi}{2}} = i  \\
e^{\frac{3i\pi}{2}} = (e^{\frac{i\pi}{2}})^2 = i^2 = -1  \\
e^{\frac{3i\pi}{2}} = (e^{\frac{i\pi}{2}})^3 = i^3 = -i  \\
e^{2i\pi}           = (e^{\frac{i\pi}{2}})^4 = i^4 = 1
$$
